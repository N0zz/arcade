Privacy Policy

Komyo Games · last updated 28 June 2026

The short version: there are no accounts and no Komyo server that stores anything about you. Your game data stays in your browser, on your device. The only third-party tracking is Google Analytics, and only if you tap Accept on the cookie banner — decline and nothing about you is collected.

1. No accounts, no server

Komyo Games is a static website — a catalogue of self-contained browser games. There are no logins or user accounts, and we don't run a database or server that stores your personal information.

2. What's stored on your device

Your game scores, progress, favorites, display name, and settings are saved in your browser's local storage, on your device only. We never receive or see this data. It stays until you clear your browser data, use Reset all data in the menu, or (where available) export/import it yourself.

3. Analytics (Google Analytics 4)

We use Google Analytics to understand which games are popular and how the site is used. It loads only after you accept the cookie banner — if you decline, no analytics script runs and no analytics cookies are set.

When enabled, Google Analytics collects standard usage data (pages viewed, approximate location from your IP address, device and browser type, and similar) and processes it on Google's servers as our data processor. We use it only in aggregate; we don't sell data and there are no ads. See Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out by declining the banner, clearing cookies, using your browser's privacy controls, or the Google Analytics opt-out add-on.

4. Cookies

The only cookies are those set by Google Analytics, and only after you consent. Your consent choice itself is remembered in local storage (not a cookie) so we don't ask again on every visit.

5. Scores shared to Discord

When you finish a game, your score — together with your display name, or "anonymous" if you haven't set one — is posted to our public Komyo Games Discord so players can see recent runs. If you'd rather stay anonymous, simply don't set a display name. (An opt-out toggle is planned.)

6. Newsletter

If you choose to subscribe, the email address you enter is sent to our email provider (Kit) to deliver occasional updates. We don't use it for anything else, and every email has an unsubscribe link.

7. External links & services

The site links out to services with their own privacy policies — including Discord, GitHub, and Buy Me a Coffee. We're not responsible for their practices.

8. Children

Komyo Games is family-friendly: no ads, no chat, no payments, and no sign-ups. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children. Since there are no accounts and nothing is stored on our servers, there is no personal data for us to hold about a child.

9. Your choices

You can decline analytics on the cookie banner, clear or reset your on-device data at any time, and unsubscribe from the newsletter whenever you like. If you're in a region with data-protection laws (e.g. GDPR or CCPA), the practical effect is the same: we hold no account or server-side personal data about you, and analytics is opt-in.

10. Contact

Questions? Use the Feedback option in the menu, email news@komyo.online, or open an issue on GitHub.

11. Changes

We may update this policy as the site evolves; the date at the top reflects the latest version.
